Why trade the financial market

Conventional investments are no longer the smart investment, nor are they providing us with the returns we need. With the rate of inflation sitting just over 5%, putting your money in conventional investments actually means that your money is worth less when it is returned to you. So why not take control of your money and earn the returns you so deserve.

What you need to start trading

All you need is a laptop and an internet connection and you're ready to go. Full time or part time, from as little as 30 minutes a day, is enough to start generating a sustainable income.

Did you know?

Did you know that many private financial traders take home R500,000 to R10m per annum - with some traders starting with less than R15,000 in their trading accounts.
